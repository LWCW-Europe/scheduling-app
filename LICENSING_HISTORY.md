# Licensing History

This project was originally developed by Rachel Weinberg, without an open-source license. In 2025, a fork was created for [LessWrong Community Weekend](https://lwcw.org) with Rachel's permission, with contributions from additional collaborators, also without a formal license.

On 2026-04-03, Rachel Weinberg added the MIT license to the project and assigned all copyright in the project to Omar Kohl.

The following contributors have explicitly confirmed their contributions may be published under the MIT license:

- Rachel Weinberg
- Omar Kohl
- Velizar Hristov
- Leonardo Taglialegne
- Nikita Brancatisano (thevinter)
- Habryka

The remaining contributors (Austin Chen, Simon Crane, Raemon, Marc N., Niklas Fasching) made small contributions and have not responded. We believe the MIT license applies to the project as a whole, but want to be transparent about this.
